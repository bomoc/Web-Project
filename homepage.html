Welcome to the sweet world!

Sisterfood offers to our dearest customers the highest quality products!

Cupcakes are great! Everybody wants to eat them all the time. Sisterfood gives to you not only tasty but also beautiful cupcakes! Good appetite and easy on the eyes! Our cupcakes look amazing! But they are not regular cupcakes! They are made from rice flour and tapioca starch to assure the Asian flavor! You can order cupackes based on your own creative design also! Just send us the pictures and we will make them ready for you!
